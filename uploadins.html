How to upload file?

Step 1

Fillup the form correctly as shown

Step 2

Click Next

Step 3

Click choosefile and select file from your device to upload. Please note that we only process STL and OBJ file formats.

Step 4

Provide your name, emailaddress and click Upload.

Step 5

Please wait until it's 100% complete. Once it's complete dDone message will appeared on screen.

Step 6 - Please skip this.

Please skip this. At this point we've already received the file. You don't need to fill any details.
